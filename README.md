# SPACE MARIO 🚀

Welcome to **Space Mario** (formerly Quantum Dreamscape) — a high-speed, infinite side-scrolling sci-fi web game!

Take control of a high-tech floating robot as it navigates the deep cosmos. Drift smoothly over giant rolling planets, blast incoming flaming asteroids with your laser, and collect golden IEEE tokens on your journey through space!

## 🎮 How to Play

### Controls
*   **[ SPACEBAR ] or [ Touch & Hold ]**: Activate your thrusters to smoothly float up. Release to gently drift back down.
*   **[ RIGHT ARROW ]**: Engage speed boost! Fly past obstacles at light-speed.
*   **[ F ] or [ Left Click ]**: Fire your blue laser cannon! 
*   **[ P ] or [ ESC ]**: Pause the game and enter **Zero-Gravity Mode**.

### Survival & Objectives
1.  **Avoid the Planets**: Absolutely do not touch the giant rolling planets! They are indestructible; you must use your thrusters to safely float over them.
2.  **Destroy the Meteorites**: Occasionally, a flaming asteroid will streak out of the sky directly at you! When time slows down and the screen zooms in, quickly fire your laser to obliterate the asteroid. Doing so safely awards you a **Diamond**. If the meteorite hits you, it's game over!
3.  **Collect IEEE Coins**: Gather the floating yellow coins featuring the IEEE logo to increase your score!
4.  **Travel Far**: The longer you survive, the faster the game naturally gets. Your score is based on the astronomical distance (AU) you travel.

## 🌟 Key Features
*   **Silky Smooth Physics**: Satisfying Mario-like jump/floating mechanics with realistic inertia and engine exhaust thrust.
*   **Zero-Gravity Pause Mode**: Pausing the game doesn't just stop the action—it transitions the entire game into a zero-g vacuum, letting you pan the camera and watch the environment slowly float by.
*   **Cinematics & Audio**: Features cinematic slow-motion "near-misses", reactive screen-shakes, deep synth audio effects on inputs, and an animated cartoon text-to-speech intro.
*   **Deep Space Visuals**: A beautiful generative space background, complete with glowing nebulae and thousands of procedural twinkling stars.

## 🛠️ Running the Game
No server or build process is required! Simply open the `index.html` file in any modern web browser (Edge, Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) to start playing immediately. Enjoy!
